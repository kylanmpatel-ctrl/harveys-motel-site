04 — Southern Oregon

Explore Medford
& the Rogue Valley

Harvey's Motel puts you right in the middle of it all. Use us as your base for everything Southern Oregon has to offer.

🌋 ~1 hr away

National Park

Crater Lake

The deepest lake in the US — born from a collapsed volcano. Brilliant cobalt-blue waters and world-class dark skies you won't forget.

🍷 Multiple nearby

Wine Country

Rogue Valley Wineries

Over 150 wineries in a Forbes-recognized global wine destination. Known for Pinot Noir, Merlot, and stunning valley scenery.

🎭 20 min to Ashland

Arts & Culture

Oregon Shakespeare Festival

One of the largest nonprofit regional theater companies in the US — world-renowned productions running February through October.

⛰️ 20 min away

Nature & Hiking

Upper & Lower Table Rock

Two dramatic flat-topped buttes above the Rogue River valley — seasonal wildflowers, raptors, and sweeping panoramic views.

🌲 5 min away

Parks & Recreation

Bear Creek Park

A beloved Medford park along Bear Creek — perfect for picnics, walking, and family time in the heart of the city.

✈️ ~10 min drive

Getting Here

Rogue Valley Airport (MFR)

Medford's regional airport is just a short drive away. Easy Uber & Lyft access from arrivals to our front door.
